About Us Welcome to TK Tharun Photography, where we capture the moments that matter the most to you. Founded by Tharunkumar C., a passionate photographer hailing from the vibrant town of Karur, our studio specializes in creating timeless memories through the art of photography.
Our Story
With a deep-rooted connection to Karur, a town renowned for its textile industry, Tharunkumar developed a keen eye for detail and beauty from a young age. This passion for aesthetics naturally evolved into a love for photography. Currently pursuing a B.E. in Electronics and Communication Engineering at M. Kumarasamy College of Engineering, Tharunkumar combines technical expertise with a creative vision to deliver stunning photographs.
